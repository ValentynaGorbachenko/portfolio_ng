HabitBreakers

Collaborated on a team of four to create an application that helps people to break their habits. Idea is to register and ask your friends or relatives to help you break your habit. Any user can have habits and ask for help by setting a fee to be payed to the helper/non-profit, or be a helper and track habit holder and submit violation.

Owned design of the database, overall architecture of the app, full CRUD operations for users and habits.

Technology used: Python, MySQL, Pylot MVC, Twilio API, HTML5, CSS, AWS

  • Group Project: Valentyna Gorbachenko, Dahye Hwang, Andrew Liu, Harmann Bajwa
  • Date: July 2016
